Help text

 AP_TO_RI_DEG   Convert amplitude/phase(deg) vectors to real/imaginary.

                AP_TO_RI_DEG(a,p) returns the real and imaginary 
                components in a two-column matrix as [real,imag].
 
                Call as: [R,I]=ap_to_ri_deg(amp,pha);
